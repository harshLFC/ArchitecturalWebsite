About Sai Consultancy

This company was formed in 2011, by our experienced team of Architects and Engineers.
We provide consultancy to MES, Arbutus, and a few industrial firms.
Apart from residential projects, we also deal with commercial and educational projects.
Innovative designs with use contemporary materials, is our specialty.
